About Me

My name is Tong Yu, a senior student majoring in Media and Art, specializing in photography and documentary filmmaking. My work captures the human experience and environmental issues through both black & white film and digital photography. I focus on documenting the lives of coastal communities in Fujian and minority groups near the Mekong River in Thailand. Through these projects, I aim to tell compelling stories that highlight social and ecological challenges, connecting people to deeper narratives of the world.
